What Are the Latest Fashion Trends?

Cheetah & Leopard Print

These two prints seem to go in and out of style, in a blink of an eye, for decades now. Well guys, it's back! A notably popular, cheetah print item bought by many were: Cheetah Print Jeans. Too, many people seemed to buy the Steve Madden Leopard Print Shoes

Headbands

Headbands are back this year. Whether it's the Y2K zig zag headband or the sporty chic headband, they are all back in style. Headbands are a great way to accessorize yourself and still look cute, espeically if you're having a bad hair day.

Skinny Jeans

This was not on my 2025 bingo card, but here we are! Skinny jeans have returned. I'm not sure if we should be excited or scared, but either way let's embrace it! Alix Earle, a popular TikTok creator and influencer, even did a colaboration with FRAME at the end of 2024, to showcase that the skinny jean is back. Skinny jeans are great at exucating curves and sleekness, also many clothing companies make their skinny jeans out of a mix between cotton and spandex, to create a sense of comfortability while wearing them.
